Privacy Policy

Ember Stopper collects personal information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, service interests, and message details. We use this information to respond to enquiries, provide quotes, organise service discussions, and communicate about wildfire home protection services.

We do not sell personal information. We may share your information with service providers who support our operations where required for communication, administration, or delivery coordination. We take reasonable steps to protect information from misuse, interference, and unauthorised access.

You can request access to the personal information we hold about you, or request a correction if details are inaccurate. To make a request, contact us at info@emberstopper.com. If you have concerns about privacy handling, contact us and we will address your request promptly.
