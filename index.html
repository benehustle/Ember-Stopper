The reality today

Wildfire risk is rising—and so is the pressure on homeowners

Wildfire preparation is not only about the fire front. Insurance markets are shifting at the same time as intensity increases.

  • Rising insurance costs — premiums are climbing sharply in many high-risk areas.
  • Reduced coverage — policies are narrower when you need them most.
  • Cover withdrawn — some households are left without a policy at all.

How do you protect your home when you cannot rely on insurance alone?
