Why Ember Stopper Exists

Ember Stopper was born from a simple but powerful idea - that homeowners should not have to choose between staying to defend their home and leaving to stay safe. The Ember Stopper system is a protective cover that unravels from the roofline and secures to the ground, forming a barrier against ember attack and radiant heat during a wildfire event.

Once deployed, you can evacuate with confidence knowing your home is protected. Developed in Australia and designed for real-world wildfire conditions, Ember Stopper is built for homeowners in bushfire-prone areas who want a practical, deployable answer - not just a plan. Because protecting houses protects neighbourhoods, and protecting neighbourhoods saves lives.
