Our Story

We started Ember Stopper in 2025 because we saw the same hard decision facing families in fire-prone areas: stay back and defend, or leave and worry about what is left behind. We believed there had to be a safer middle path. Our answer was a deployable cover that unravels from the roofline and secures to the ground, creating a practical barrier against ember attack and radiant heat during wildfire events.

We built the system in Australia for real conditions, not a showroom scenario. We speak plainly, plan carefully, and help homeowners prepare before summer pressure arrives. Our work is about helping people leave early with confidence, knowing they have taken meaningful protective steps. For us, this is bigger than one property. When homes are better protected, streets are safer, neighbourhoods are stronger, and recovery after a fire event is faster for everyone.
