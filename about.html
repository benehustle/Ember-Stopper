Our story

Insurance is no longer guaranteed—neither is “wait and see”

Ember Stopper was created to solve a problem many homeowners face in wildfire-prone areas. When conditions rise, families are often forced into a painful choice: stay and defend—or leave and worry about what is left behind.

At the same time, a growing number of households are facing another reality: premiums that hurt, policies that shrink, or cover that disappears altogether. We believed there had to be a better option than crossing fingers.

What we created

A deployable layer you control

Ember Stopper is a deployable home protection system that allows you to take action before a fire arrives: protect the home externally, follow clear steps, and leave earlier without second-guessing whether you “did enough.”

It is designed to reduce ember entry and radiant heat exposure along the roofline-to-ground envelope—especially valuable when insurance cannot be your safety net.

Built in Australia

Designed for real conditions

We developed Ember Stopper in Australia, where bushfire demands practical solutions, clear decision-making, and early action. Everything we do is grounded in field-level thinking—not theory.

That same mindset travels with us for homeowners in other high-risk regions who need the same clarity.
